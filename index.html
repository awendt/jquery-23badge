jquery-23badge by awendt

Show off your photos on 23hq.com

Download

You can download this project in either zip or tar formats.

You can also clone the project with Git by running:

$ git clone git://github.com/awendt/jquery-23badge

Usage

By default, the widget shows 10 of your 50 most recent photos at random. If you want it to display the most recent 10 photos, you're welcome to send me a patch.

You'll need your user_id from 23 Photo Sharing. (Note: This is not your username that you use for logging into the site!) In fact, let me get that for you:
